# Writing Plans Optimized

Plan until the next action is obvious, not until every detail is frozen.

## Flow

1. State goal, constraints, and non-goals.
2. Inspect existing structure before proposing changes.
3. Break work into ordered, verifiable steps.
4. Mark risks, open questions, and rollback points.
5. Convert the plan into a short execution checklist.

## Gotchas

- Do not plan from assumptions when files can be inspected.
- Do not hide uncertainty.
- Do not make a plan that cannot be verified.
