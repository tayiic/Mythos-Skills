# Brainstorming Optimized

Separate exploration from commitment.

## Flow

1. Restate the problem and success criteria.
2. Generate 3-5 materially different options.
3. Compare tradeoffs, risks, cost, and reversibility.
4. Recommend one path with assumptions.
5. Mark what would change the recommendation.

## Gotchas

- Do not present tiny variants as real options.
- Do not over-plan before the problem is framed.
- Do not hide assumptions.
