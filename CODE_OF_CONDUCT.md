# Code Of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Standard

We want Mythos-Skills to be useful, rigorous, and welcoming. Good contributions can challenge ideas directly while treating people with respect.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Clear technical disagreement.
- Careful attribution.
- Constructive review.
- Patient explanation for new contributors.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- License removal or attribution stripping.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban contributors who violate this standard.
